Problem

We need the best care for COVID-19 patients.

Unclear treatment guidelines

Doctors and nurses want to know that they are providing the best possible care to their hospitalized COVID19 patients, but research is moving too slowly to generate the findings needed for top-notch, evidence-based medicine.

Unstructured data collection

The clinical treatment of COVID-19 has become decentralized non-controlled, creating the inefficiencies in care delivery. To facilitates design of clinical trials, we need high quality observational data to accelerate treatment protocol development.

Unresponsive System Design

There is no global clinical database can inform clinician in real time. We need improve preparedness for future outbreaks.

Solution

A curated COVID-19 registry database of patient-level data from US hospitals that can be accessed by researchers to answer questions about the best treatment options for patients with specific characteristics.

COVIData Registry


collect data on every patient hospitalized


complete master list of implemented cares


curated database of patient-level data


Standardized, high-quality, real-time data collected by hospital professionals

Data Analysis

Evaluate the effectiveness of care delivery during the COVID-19 crisis with up to date clinical data.

Personalized Care

Patient Care of COVID-19 would be greatly enhanced because clinical pathways designed from COVIData registry can share broadly.
